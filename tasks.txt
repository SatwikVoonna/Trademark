    Build a semantic search web app for NCERT Science textbooks (Class 6 to 11).

## App Purpose
Users type a natural language query (e.g. "how do plants make food") and get
back the most relevant passages from NCERT Science textbooks, ranked by
semantic similarity.

## Layout & Pages
- Single page app
- Top: App title "SciSearch" with a tagline "Search NCERT Science, Semantically"
- Center: A large search bar with a search button
- Below search: Quick-suggestion chips (e.g. Photosynthesis, Gravity, Atoms)
- Results section: Cards showing matched passages

## Result Card Design
Each card should show:
- Class number badge (e.g. "Class 8")
- Chapter name as card title
- Matched passage text with query keywords highlighted
- Similarity score as a thin progress bar (0–100%)
- Subject tag (Physics / Chemistry / Biology)
